Laptop Viewer

Use this mode on your laptop to view the phone's camera feed:

  1. Click "Start as Receiver"
  2. Enter the connection ID from your phone
  3. Click "Connect"

The phone must already be in sender mode.

×

Phone Camera

Use this mode on your phone to share its camera:

  1. Click "Start as Sender"
  2. Allow camera access
  3. Scan the QR code or share the connection URL with your laptop

Keep this page open during use.
